How Item Levels Are Calculated

Each slot is scored on three dimensions then multiplied by a quality factor

The Formula

ITEM LEVEL = (Maturity × 0.4) + (Coverage × 0.3) + (Integration × 0.3) × Quality Multiplier Quality Multipliers: Legendary × 1.15 Epic × 1.05 Rare × 1.00 Missing × 0.00

40% Weight Maturity

How battle-tested is this component? Is it actively used on every session? Does it have documented failure modes and known edge cases? A score of 100 means it has never failed in production use.

30% Weight Coverage

What percentage of relevant use cases does this slot handle? Are there known gaps or blind spots? A score of 100 means every scenario this slot should address is handled with no missing functionality.

30% Weight Integration

How well does this component communicate with others? Are loops actually closed, or merely theoretical? A score of 100 means every adjacent slot can read, write, and act on this component's outputs.
